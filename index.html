About Us

About

RCE Star News is a member of various media organizations, delivering accurate and timely news to the people of Pagadian City and Zamboanga del Sur.

RCE Start news is an accredited media organization

We are accredited by the provincial government of Zamboanga del Sur, and the city of Pagadian through our membership with the Zamboanga del Sur Pagadian City Press Club Inc.(ZPPI).

  • Member of Philippine Press Institute (PPI)
  • Member of Publishers Association of the Philippines Inc.(PAPI)
  • Member of National Press Club of the Philippines (NPCP)
  • Member of Pagadian City and Zamboanga del Sur Media Association (PAZAMA)
