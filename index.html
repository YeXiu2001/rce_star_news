About Us

About RCE Star News - Your Trusted Pagadian City News Media

RCE Star News is an accredited news media organization and member of various press associations, delivering accurate and timely local news coverage to the people of Pagadian City and Zamboanga del Sur.

RCE Star News - Accredited Local News Provider for Pagadian City

We are an officially accredited news media organization recognized by the provincial government of Zamboanga del Sur and the city of Pagadian through our membership with the Zamboanga del Sur Pagadian City Press Club Inc. (ZPPI).

  • Member of Philippine Press Institute (PPI)
  • Member of Pagadian City and Zamboanga del Sur Media Association (PAZAMA)
  • Member of Zamboanga del Sur Pagadian City Press Club Inc.(ZPPI)
